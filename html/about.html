Location

Our school is designed to create a safe and happy environment for the growth and education of our children. Everything was put together with children in mind. The whole facility is bright and colorful with plenty of natural light. A beautiful creative mural and the aquarium keeps children interested, excited, and fascinated in the reception area. All classes have windows looking in so parents and staff can observe the children. Classrooms are designed for all age groups, and they are color themed. There’s always time for play. Our school has 2 playgrounds outdoors for different age groups and one indoor play area. The equipment has been chosen with safety in mind and they are age appropriate for all the children. Rubber material is used so children can play and run safely.

Nutrition

Here at CCC we understand that a healthy diet is critical to physical and mental childhood development. We have spent a significant amount of time researching all healthy organic and natural choices that are good for our children. Organic Life stands above the rest in this very important campaign for healthier children. We know that this company is the best partner and provider of meals for our precious children.

Curriculum

We know that children learn best when they're actively engaged. Children have the opportunity to discover their own special talents and best ways to learn while feeling valued for who they are, while having the guidance and freedom to learn in meaningful ways. We believe that all children are talented, capable, and special, whether they enjoy music or math. We know they learn Science, Math, Reading and Writing concepts through hands-on, sensory-oriented activities. Creative Kidz’ unique curriculum gives children the guidance and freedom to construct their own body of knowledge in ways most meaningful to them.
